Programs

 

The "Education of the Future" Academy is a young and open educational platform that has recently started its activities, but already unites a number of interesting projects and programs. Most of them have three age directions - for children, youth and adults and are completely free. Among the most popular programs are:

 

 

  1. Foreign languages.

 

Our conversation clubs are an online and offline space for communication, learning and cultural exchange from the most popular languages ​​of the world: German, English, French, Spanish, Chinese, Arabic, Russian and Ukrainian.

 

This is a friendly space where everyone can develop their language skills - from live communication to confident command of grammar and writing. Here you can start learning a language from scratch or improve existing knowledge to a professional level. Each participant chooses a convenient format and pace of study - from basic to advanced - and also has the opportunity to prepare for international exams of levels B1, B2 and C1, in order to confidently use the language in studies, travel and work.

 

 

  2. Digital literacy 360°

 

 

This is a practical online course for anyone who wants to confidently navigate the world of modern technology.

 

You will learn how to use a computer, smartphone and key online services: create accounts, work safely with passwords, book tickets, search for housing, work or information on the Internet, communicate through modern platforms and protect your data from cyber threats.

 

A separate block is devoted to artificial intelligence - how it already helps in everyday life, work and study. You will learn how to use ChatGPT, virtual assistants, automation tools and smart services to make your life more convenient, safer and more productive.

 

 

  3. Your own business from scratch

 

This is a step-by-step online course for those who dream of starting their own business. The program is designed for beginners who want to understand where to start, how to choose an idea, test its viability, register a business and organize the first sales. You will get a clear idea of ​​various business models - from online services and freelancing to small businesses in the field of trade, education or creative industries.

 

The course includes separate sections for different European countries - Germany, Poland, France, Spain and England. Each block is devoted to practical aspects of doing business in this country: registration, taxes, permits, marketing, cultural features and government support. This allows you to choose the right direction and get specific knowledge for a real start to your own business in the chosen European country.

 

 

  4. IT school of programming and design.

 

This is a modern online space where you will master the key skills of the digital age. The program covers the most popular areas: programming, web development, design, artificial intelligence, data analysis and cybersecurity.

 

You will learn how to work with the most common languages ​​and technologies - Python, Java, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, SQL, C++ - and you will be able to create your own projects, websites and applications from scratch.

 

Separate modules are devoted to AI technologies, UX/UI design, automation and robotization of processes, as well as working with modern digital tools and frameworks such as React, TensorFlow, Node.js. The training is built practically and accessible - to help everyone find their direction, develop technical thinking and confidently take a step into the world of modern IT professions.

 

  5. Space "Creators of the Future"

 

 

This is more than a training course - it's a living community for everyone without exception. Here we don't just learn - we create together. Each participant is both a student and a mentor, because true development begins when you share knowledge.

 

We get to know ourselves - we discover our own values, strengths and life guidelines. We set goals and get inspired - we formulate realistic but motivating steps to change. We manage time and life - we plan, prioritize, find a balance between work, study and rest. We develop thinking - we train attention, memory, creativity and logic.

 

We grow professionally - we build a career, look for a vocation, realize our potential. We maintain energy and harmony - we learn not to burn out, to maintain inner peace and confidence. We develop emotional maturity - we understand ourselves and interact with others consciously and with respect.

 

Together we create success and the future - consciously, harmoniously, inspired.
